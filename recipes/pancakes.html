Scotch Pancakes

Description

Fluffy, sweet and filling. The BEST way to start your day!
For best results, make the batter the night before and refrigerate.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 300ml Semi-Skimmed Milk
  • 200g Plain Flour
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tbsp Caster Sugar
  • Olive Oil (for cooking)
  • Maple Syrup

Method

  1. Add the Flour, Baking Powder, Cinnamon and Sugar to a large bowl and mix together.
  2. Make a hole in the middle of the dry ingredients and crack the egg into the hole
  3. Gently beat the Egg in a circular motion into the surrounding flour. Add 100ml of
    Milk once the mixture looks thick. Do this twice more until all the milk is in the mixture
    and you have a smooth, silky batter.
  4. Heat a drizzle of Olive Oil in a large frying pan on medium-high heat.
  5. Once hot, lower the heat and add two ladles of batter seperately into the pan
  6. When you notice bubbles appearing in the batter, flip each pancake over with a
    Fish Slice and heat for another 30s-1min before removing to a plate.
  7. Repeat the process until you have the required number of pancakes, then serve
    a stack to each person, pour over the syrup and enjoy!
  8. Store any remaining batter in the fridge for up to 3 days.
